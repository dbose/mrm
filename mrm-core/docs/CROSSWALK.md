## Notes

This crosswalk identifies conceptual equivalence across four major model
risk management regulatory frameworks. Mappings reflect substantive
alignment, not perfect semantic equivalence. Empty arrays indicate no
corresponding requirement in that standard.

Use case: Financial institutions operating in multiple jurisdictions can
use this crosswalk to demonstrate compliance with multiple standards using
a unified validation approach.

Limitations: Standards evolve; this crosswalk reflects standards as of
May 2026. Regulatory interpretations may differ across jurisdictions.
Consult with legal/compliance before relying on these mappings for
regulatory submissions.
